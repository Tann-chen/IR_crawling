Concordia University Press books are engaged, accessible, and make a point. The Press publishes in English and in French. Concordia University Press launched on 27 October 2016. The Press is interested in proposals for books and pamphlets in the arts, humanities, and social sciences that engage with the themes of Life, Knowledge, and Creation.

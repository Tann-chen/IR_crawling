 www.concordia.ca/giving: Critical research into sustainable energy and smart-grid security is receiving a major boost at Concordia, thanks to a $3.9 million donation from Hydro-Québec announced October 12, 2017. In addition to supporting three research chairs, the generous gift aims to attract top students to the university by providing 68 merit-based student awards.

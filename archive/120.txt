It shares this responsibility with the faculty associations, which represent students according to Concordia’s four faculties: - Fine Arts Students Alliance (FASA)- Arts & Science Federation of Associations (ASFA) 
- Commerce and Administration Students’ Association (CASA)
- Engineering and Computer Science Association (ECA)  The Faculty Associations are not formally affiliated with the CSU, but are its equals and partners in representing and serving Concordia’s undergraduate students.

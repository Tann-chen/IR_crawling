 Jury                                                                      Kanita Ahmed is a board member of the Kabir Centre and works at McGill University. She is passionate about South Asian art, literature and cinema. Dr. Alan Wong is a professor in the English Dept. at Vanier College and holds a Ph.D. from Concordia University. Dr. Jill Didur is the Graduate Program Director and Professor in the Department of English at Concordia University. Akhil, Vice President of the board of Kabir Centre, is a business executive and has wide-ranging interests in literature, cinema and performing arts. He is interested in promoting South Asian arts and culture to mainstream audiences in Canada.

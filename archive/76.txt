    |         JMAC offers a variety of academic workshops that relate to different topics of student interest such as career planning, stress management, body language and much more!  Like our Facebook page to find more about our workshops and other services!   JMAC provides academic services and events to all undergraduate students, such as the Peer Mentorship Program, Groups, Online Lecture Program, Online Book Exchange, Calculator/I-Clicker Rental Program and the Internal Case Competition.   JMAC is a student run organization operating as a subsidiary of the Commerce and Administration Students’ Association (CASA). We believe that it is a privilege to be involved within our school community and be given the opportunity to enhance the university experience of JMSB students.

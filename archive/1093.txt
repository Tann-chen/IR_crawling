 Urban Futures: Developing Conscientious Competition ProposalsDART498 Guest Speaker Series – Summer 2017Technical Direction and Public Art ProductionAntoine Clayette and Steve Blanchette, Wireframe Studio – May 30, 2017Wireframe Studio principals Antoine Clayette and Steve Blanchette speak about the process of working with artists to produce public art projects.DART498 was the summer incubator course for the Legado Public Art Competition offered by the department of Design and Computation Arts, in association with the Institute for Urban Future’s City Bureau. A series of practitioners and experts were invited to speak in class on various aspects of developing public art competition proposals. The course was taught by Moana Andraos and Melissa Mongiat.
